Slow Cooker Baby Back Ribs

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds baby back ribs, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • Â½ onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1. Season ribs with salt and pepper.
  2. 2. Pour 1/2 cup water into the slow cooker, then add ribs. Scatter onion and garlic over top.
  3. 3. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours or High for 4 hours.
  4. 4. When the ribs are almost finished, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. 5. Transfer ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet and coat with barbeque sauce. Discard onion and garlic.
  6. 6. Bake ribs in the preheated oven until sauce caramelizes and sticks to meat, 10 to 15 minutes.
