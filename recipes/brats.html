Beer Brats

Ingredients

  • 4 (12 ounce) cans beer
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 bratwurst
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1. Combine beer and onion slices in a large pot; bring to a boil. Add bratwurst, pepper flakes, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. 2. Meanwhile, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. 3. Remove bratwurst to a plate. Reduce heat to low and let onions simmer until needed.
  4. 4. Cook bratwurst on the preheated grill, turning occasionally to get char marks, until no longer pink in the middle, 5 to 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  5. 5. Serve bratwurst with onions.
